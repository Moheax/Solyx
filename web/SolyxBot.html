What is Solyx.

A text based RPG, with in depth lore about monsters, locations and items, classes with specialization, PvP battles, lots of weapons and armour, and earnable titles.

Commands

To begin
------------------------------------------------------------------

-begin
a character to start your adventure!

Fighting

------------------------------------------------------------------

-fight

Fight a monster

-hp

Check your health

-heal

Use a healing potion to gain HP

-travel

Go to a new location to fight different monsters

Inventory & Items

------------------------------------------------------------------

-inv / -inventory

Check your inventory

-stats

See your statistics

-profile

Show your profile card

-rank

Show your rank card

-market

Sell and buy items on the market

-equip

Equip a weapon or a piece of armor

-sell

Sell an item

-crate

Open a crate

-mine

Mine for stone and metal

-chop

Chop for wood

-fish

Fishing command
